Brief Intro

I am a second-year Computer Science student at Sheffield Hallam University currently looking for a placement between my second and third years.

Why I studied Computer Science

I have been interested in information technologies from an early age. I knew I wanted to study in the broad area of information technology as it would enable me to pursue a career in an area I greatly enjoy.

I chose to study for a degree in Computer Science degree in particular as it provides me with a wide set of skills and knowledge which form the foundation for many systems and technologies. The broad knowledge I gained would allow me to specialise as required at a future date.

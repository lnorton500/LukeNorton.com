Brief Intro

Being just under the Tyne is quite useful as it means I can just get a twenty minute bus and I’m in the City centre of Newcastle which is great for going out with my friends.

I went to Lord Lawson of Beamish Academy in a town called Birtley for my secondary school education and then stayed on to Sixth Form where I studied Maths, Physics and IT BTEC.
